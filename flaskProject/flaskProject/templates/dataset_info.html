Dataset Information

Feature Description
age_at_diagnosis Patient age at diagnosis.
type_of_breast_surgery The type of surgery in which the breast cancer tumor is removed.
cancer_type The type of cancer (cancer or sarcoma).
cancer_type_detailed Breast cancer type (contains 5 different types).
cellularity Data on cancer cellularity examined after chemotherapy.
chemotherapy Whether the patient received chemotherapy during the disease process or not.
pam50_+_claudin-low_subtype Contains data obtained as a result of examining 50 different gene regions.
cohort Groups of subjects who share an identifying feature (values from 1 to 5 in the dataset).
er_status_measured_by_ihc Data evaluating the measurement status of estrogen receptors.
er_status Estrogen receptors of cancer cells.
neoplasm_histologic_grade The pathology of the cells is examined. Determine whether he appears aggressive or not.

tumor_other_histologic_subtype Result of cancer type obtained by microscopic examination.
hormone_therapy Data on whether the patient is receiving hormone therapy.
inferred_menopausal_state The patient's menopausal status.
integrative_cluster The molecular subtype of cancer.
primary_tumor_laterality Information about whether the cancer is in the right breast or the left breast.
lymph_nodes_examined_positive Information on whether there is cancer in the lymph node as a result of the examination of the patient's lymph nodes.
mutation_count The number of genes with mutations.
nottingham_prognostic_index Data used to determine prognosis after surgery.
oncotree_code An open-source ontology.
overall_survival_months Patient's survival time.
overall_survival Data whether the patient is alive or not.
pr_status Progesterone receptor status of cancer cells.
radio_therapy Information about whether the patient is using radiotherapy.
3-gene_classifier_subtype Information about the 3 gene classifier subtypes.
tumor_size The size of the tumor.
tumor_stage Stage of the tumor.
